Cauliflower Curry

This is what the Cauliflower Curry will look like when complete

This is a simple and delicious vegetarian curry recipe that is one of my favorites. You can also make it with potatoes, broccoli, carrots, or any of your other favorite veggies.

Ingredients

  • 1 Whole Cauliflower
  • 1/4 tsp Turmeric (ground)
  • 1/4 tsp Salt
  • 1/4 tsp Freshly Ground Pepper
  • 1 Tsp Sunflower Oil
  • 2 Onions
  • 8-10 Cashews (raw)
  • A Little Ginger
  • 2 Tomatoes
  • 4 Garlic Cloves
  • 1 tsp Garam Masala
  • 1 tsp Chilli Powder
  • 10 fl oz Water
  • 1/2 tsp Sugar
